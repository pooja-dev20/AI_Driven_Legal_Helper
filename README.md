Justimind is an AI-driven platform designed to make Indian laws accessible. It translates complex legal jargon into simple, actionable guidance in regional languages, empowering citizens to understand their rights and make informed decisions.

### Features

* **Multilingual Voice & Text**: Submit legal queries in your native language via voice or text.
* **Simplified Legal Guidance**: Complex laws (IPC, CrPC) explained in plain, everyday language.
* **Lawyer Advice Verification**: A tool to cross-check and verify advice received from legal professionals.
* **Legality Checker**: Instantly analyze if a proposed action is legal or illegal under Indian law.
* **Document Analyzer**: Upload and summarize legal documents to identify key risks and clauses.
* **Emergency Assistance**: Priority processing for urgent legal situations with 10-second response times.
* **Legal Aid Referral**: Direct connection to relevant NGOs and pro bono legal services.
* **Privacy First**: Secure data encryption with options for completely anonymous usage.

### Tech Stack

* **Frontend**: React.js, Flutter, Web Speech API.
* **Backend**: Node.js, Python (FastAPI/Flask).
* **AI/ML**: Large Language Models (LLMs), NLP, and Regional Translation Engines.
* **Cloud & DB**: AWS (Hosting), PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and Pinecone (Vector Search).
